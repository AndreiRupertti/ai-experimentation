🚀 Quick Start

  • Double-click on canvas to create a new component
  • Click a component to select it
  • Ctrl+click a component to duplicate it
  • Drag & drop components to organize hierarchy
  • Click the ▸/▾ to expand/collapse components
  • Connection Mode: Click 🔗 Connect button or press 'C' key, then click two components to create arrows. Press Esc to exit.
  • Share: Click the 🔗 Share button to copy a shareable URL of your current canvas
